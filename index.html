Lil' Nibbles Alpha

Our debut title is a charming puzzle-adventure game. This section introduces the core concepts of the game. On the right, you can interact with a simple canvas element that captures the playful spirit of being a mouse on a mission.

The Premise

Players take on the role of a clever little mouse in a mysterious room. The objective isn't immediately obvious; it's up to you to explore, interact, and uncover the ultimate goal.

The Gameplay

The core challenge is to piece together clues and solve environmental puzzles. A central hint guides the player: find the cheese and figure out how to get it back home.
